# Stride — Product Roadmap (Q2–Q3 2026)

*Last updated: April 2026 | Owner: Growth & Onboarding PM*

---

## Now (Q2 2026 — April–June)

### OB-1: Guided Setup Flow ⚠️ IN PROGRESS
**What:** Replace blank-slate onboarding with a 5-step guided setup that helps teams configure Stride for their workflow in under 10 minutes.
**Why:** 60% of signups never complete setup. Exit surveys say "didn't know where to start."
**Success metric:** Setup completion rate from 40% → 70%
**Status:** Design complete. Engineering kicked off week of April 7.

### OB-2: Team Invite Nudge
**What:** Proactive prompts to invite teammates during setup (currently optional, buried in settings).
**Why:** Teams that invite 3+ members in week 1 have 4x higher 90-day retention.
**Success metric:** % of new teams with 3+ members at day 7: from 18% → 40%
**Status:** Not started. Waiting for OB-1 to ship.

### INT-1: Slack Integration (Read-only)
**What:** Post Alignment Hub updates directly to Slack channels.
**Why:** Top requested feature from churned users (Dev persona). Reduces "I never saw the update" problem.
**Success metric:** 25% of paying teams enable Slack integration in first 30 days.
**Status:** In progress. Beta with 5 teams.

---

## Next (Q3 2026 — July–September)

### OB-3: Onboarding Checklist (In-app)
**What:** Persistent checklist visible to all team members (not just PM) showing team setup progress.
**Why:** Adoption blockers are often non-PM team members who don't know what to do.
**Success metric:** Time-to-first-value from 11 days → 5 days.
**Status:** Planned.

### OB-4: Role-Based Onboarding Paths
**What:** Separate first-run experience for PMs, EMs, and stakeholders — each sees the features most relevant to their role.
**Why:** Persona research shows EMs and stakeholders churn because they see PM-only features first and feel lost.
**Success metric:** 14-day retention for non-PM users from 31% → 50%.
**Status:** Planned.

### AI-1: Decision Draft Assist
**What:** PM types a quick note, Gemini drafts a structured Decision Log entry with problem, options, decision, and rationale.
**Why:** Decision Log has high perceived value but low usage — "takes too long to write."
**Success metric:** Decision Log entries per team per month from 2 → 8.
**Status:** Spec in progress.
