# Stride — Company Overview

## What We Do
Stride is a product management platform for distributed teams. We help PMs, engineers, and stakeholders stay aligned across roadmaps, sprints, and decisions — without the meeting overhead.

Think: the structured workflow of Jira, the flexibility of Notion, and the speed of Linear — built for remote-first product teams.

## The Problem We're Solving
Most PM tools are built for the PM, not the team. The result: PMs live in their tool, engineers ignore it, and stakeholders get PDFs.

Stride fixes this with role-based views — every stakeholder sees exactly what they need, synced from a single source of truth.

---

## Core Product Areas

### 1. Roadmap Studio
Drag-and-drop roadmap builder with timeline, kanban, and dependency views. Syncs bi-directionally with Jira and Linear.

### 2. Decision Log
Structured space to document product decisions with context, tradeoffs, and outcomes. Searchable. Versioned.

### 3. Alignment Hub
Stakeholder update generator — pulls from roadmap and decision log, auto-drafts weekly updates by audience (exec, eng, sales).

### 4. Sprint Bridge
Connects PM priorities to engineering sprints. No more copy-pasting tickets between tools.
