## Key Themes (Raw — Not Yet Synthesized)

- **Team adoption is the #1 churn driver** — PMs sign up, teams don't follow
- **Setup friction** — too long, too many decisions, unclear first value
- **Integration gaps** — Jira sync and Slack posting are table stakes
- **Onboarding is PM-only** — engineers, designers, and execs feel excluded
- **No ongoing guidance** — users feel lost after initial setup completes
- **Invite flow is broken** — invite emails going to spam, low open rate
- **Mobile experience** — frequently mentioned but low volume
- **ROI unclear** — hard to justify to CFO/leadership without clear metrics
